-Edita photography-

New babies are absolutely fascinating. It’s amazing that they can be inside their mom one minute and be here among the rest of us the next. They are so miniature and so perfect…they’re simply beautiful. I can’t get enough of them! Having a new baby come into your life is a very momentous occasion and people are willing to capture those first precious moments in beautiful images that will last a lifetime.

I am Edita Paluri, Lithuanian maternity and baby photographer, settled in one of the finest Indian cities – Pune. You would probably expect me to say I am wonderful, creative and passionate photographer with mountains of experience and piling awards in the drawers. Well, most of all, I am just another hard working, creative mommy, whose hobby turned into exciting journey of fascinating career. Another You-could be Me in the past and Me-could be You in near future.

I do not believe in modern cliché of a superwoman, nor do I have any colored cape or a cat mask.


I do not wear much of the diamonds, run in Victoria’s Secrets or smell like Chanel. I know, nothing like typical photo geek from the movies! My biggest brand, perfume and salon is Canon, most precious jewels – 2 adorable kids and the only accessories – lenses .

My inspiration is Life – the very beginning of it in the womb and beautiful flourishing of each birth. My passion – to capture this magical proof of continuity via clicks of amazing Moms, fragile newborns and champion children.

I spend days chasing arrows of 24/7, learning, crafting new ideas, capturing adorable people, designing and editing photos and thinking of my to-dos. I constantly scribble images in my mind of unique set ups, make the list of teeny-veeny pieces to transform it into the picture, open my “third eye” and start clicking.

The thing I love most about my Job – wonderful people I meet every day, who are the most important, creative and combining elements of each photo-shoot and my personal success.

Cheers to you all!
